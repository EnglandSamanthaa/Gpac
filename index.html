Welcome to the 2023 - 2024 School Year

As we embark on a new school year, we'd like to welcome you to the Geneseo Performing Arts Council, as known as GPAC.

GPAC is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting the arts, including band, choir, speech, and theatre, in the Geneseo School District from kindergarten to 12th grade. If your child is involved in any of these programs, you are automatically a member of GPAC!

We gather on the first Thursday of each month at 6:30 PM in the high school choir room, and we wholeheartedly invite everyone to join us. It's a fantastic way to connect with our directors and fellow parents, and we promise, we have a great time together!

How GPAC Helps

Annually, GPAC commits over $30,000 to bolster our arts programs, and this doesn't even account for the countless volunteer hours our community donates. We raise funds through various events and greatly rely on volunteers to ensure their success. For the 2022-2023 school year, our budget surpasses $40,000, and this doesn't include the $10,000 already spent on the new trailer and sound cart.

Here's a brief overview of some recent GPAC contributions:

  • $13,000+ in support of the Middle School music programs
  • $1,500 in support of the Elementary Music programs
  • $10,000+ in support of the High School music programs
  • $8,000 in support of Marching Band/Winter Drum Line
  • $900 in support of the Speech program
  • $3,500 in scholarships for Middle and High School Students pursuing arts-related endeavors

Thank You, American Family Insurance & Tim Clifton Agency Inc.!

The Geneseo Performing Arts Program would like to say thank you to American Family Insurance & the Clifton Family for their financial gift that was used to purchase a new enclosed trailer.

Our program has grown beyond the capabilities of our existing trailer. Because of that, we have had to rely on borrowing a trailer and/or using parents vehicles to transport uniforms and equipment to and from performances.

This new trailer will allow us to travel without the restriction and burden of relying on multiple vehicles. Thank you so much for your support and generosity.
